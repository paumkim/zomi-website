## The Big Idea

Zomi is spoken as chunks but often written as syllables.
**Merge everything that belongs together.**

```
mi te    →  mite
paai ding  →  paaiding
om ta    →  omta
Zeisu in →  Zeisu'n
ki khawm →  kikhawm
```
