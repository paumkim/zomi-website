## Submit Your Church

Is your Zomi church missing from this list? Help us grow the directory.

**To submit:**
1. Open an issue or pull request at [github.com/paumkim/zomi-website](https://github.com/paumkim/zomi-website)
2. Or email your church details (name, location, denomination, contact) to the repository maintainer
3. Or simply tell a community member who can submit on your behalf

**What to include:**
- Church name
- City and country
- Denomination (Baptist, Catholic, Pentecostal, etc.)
- Affiliation (CBC, ZBCM, independent, etc.)
- Service times (optional)
- Contact information (optional)
