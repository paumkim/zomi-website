## Source

The Bible text comes from two sources:
1. **Laisiangtho Scripture** – `tedim1932.json` (30,715 verses)
2. **Laisiangtho Core** – `tedim.xml` (same translation with book descriptions)

Both are the same 1932 Tedim translation. The merged version combines them
with duplicates removed.
