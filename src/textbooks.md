# Golden Textbooks

## Zolai Simbu – The Zomi Textbook Series

These are original Zolai educational textbooks from the 1980s (Tedim Township, Myanmar),
used when the Zomi language was actively taught in schools. They represent the
**golden standard** of Zomi spelling and grammar.

> *"Before these textbooks, Zomi was written inconsistently. After them, there was
> a standard that all schools followed."*

---

## Textbook Levels

| Level | Description | Pages |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| 🧸 **Tan Lang** (Kindergarten) | First exposure to Zolai letters | 48 |
| 🟢 **Tan Khat** (Grade 1) | Basic reading and vocabulary | 54 |
| 🟢 **Tan Nih** (Grade 2) | Stories and sentence building | 62 |
| 🟡 **Tan Thum** (Grade 3) | Longer narratives and comprehension | 61 |
| 🔵 **Tan Li** (Grade 4) | Advanced reading and writing | 78 |

## Download

All textbooks and extracted text are available in the
[zomi-dataset](https://github.com/paumkim/zomi-dataset) repository under
[`Sinna/`](https://github.com/paumkim/zomi-dataset/tree/main/Sinna):

- **PDFs**: Organized by level in `Sinna/pdf/`
- **Raw OCR text**: `Sinna/text/raw/` (original spelling preserved)
- **Normalized text**: `Sinna/text/normalized/` (modern merge rules applied)
